Ultimate Steak Feast
Notes
One of my first dinners I started making, this version is a healthier and protein dense version. Highly recommend making a dipping sauce consisting of avocado, onions, jalapeno, tomato and lime!
9/10
Ultimate Steak Feast
Ingredients:
  • Ribeye Steak
  • Asparagus
  • Avocado
  • 2 Fried Eggs
  • 2 Lime
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Jalapeno
  • Onions
Steps:
  1. Cook Medium-Rare Steak
  2. Sliced Avocado on Plate
  3. Grilled Asparagus
  4. Sauce - Onions, Jalapeno, Tomatoes, Avocado, and 2 Limes
  5. Fried Eggs on Plate
  6. Add Sauce beside the Steak and enjoy

Josh's Classic Pasta
Ingredients:
  • 6 Cloves of Garlic
  • Half an Onion Diced
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Tomato Basil Sauce from Trader Joes
  • White Mushrooms
  • Fresh Basil
  • *Extra - Ricotta
Steps:
  1. Olive Oil Pan and get Garlic Golden Brown
  2. Take Garlic off and add Diced Onions
  3. Add Tomatoes and shortly after the Sauce
  4. Be cooking your pasta while doing the Sauce, make sure to add Salt!
  5. Add Garlic back in
  6. Put pasta into the Pan and top with Fresh Basil and Ricotta
  7. ***Add Meatballs for more Protein/Calories

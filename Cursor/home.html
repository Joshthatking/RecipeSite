About This Recipe Archive
Welcome to the Maxxed Out Recipe Archive! This collection of recipes have been made routinely by myself for the past couple of years. I have slowly added to my usual dinners and snacks that I enjoy for flavor and for the macros. This site is to archive my favorite things I make and be able to keep track of the recipes and macros in a cool way as a programming project. Enjoy!

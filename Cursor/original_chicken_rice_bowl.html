Original Chicken Rice Bowl
Ingredients:
  • Chicken Breast
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • One Egg
  • Chili Glaze
  • Honey
  • Ketchup
  • Flour/Corn Starch/Potato Starch
Steps:
  1. Dice Chicken Breast
  2. Add to Mixing bowl with the Soy Sauce, Egg and Seasonings
  3. Put in container and add Flour and shake!
  4. Put in air fryer @ 405F for 20 minutes - flip at 10
  5. Create Sauce by adding Chili Glaze, Soy Sauce, Ketchup, Honey and Water in pan
  6. Add Chicken once done and let it finish cooking!

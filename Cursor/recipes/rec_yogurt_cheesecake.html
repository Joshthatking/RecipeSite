Yogurt Cheesecake
Notes
This is a light, high-protein cheesecake. Perfect for a light late night snack
7/10
Yogurt Cheesecake
Ingredients:
  • 1.5 cups of Greek Yogurt
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 Tbsp of Honey/Maple Syrup/Agave
  • 2 Tbsp of Oat Flour
  • Blueberries!
Steps:
  1. Stir in bowl
  2. Put in the baking pan
  3. Oven @ 340F for 30 minutes
  4. Makes 4 slices!
